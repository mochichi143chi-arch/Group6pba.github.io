2nd Solution;

  1. Will this directly fix or reduce the root cause? Dost Green packing can help reduce the problem by using eco-friendly or recyclable packing. It helps reduce plastic pollution and environmental damage, but it does not completely eliminate waste, it only reduces it.
  2. Is it doable with the available time and resources? It is doable because the materials used are affordable and available, and it can be done within a short period of time if planned properly.
  3. Who will carry it out (teacher, students, group)? It can be carried out by students as a group, with teamwork and proper planning, under the supervision and guidance of the teacher.
  4. How long will it take? It will take years, due to lack of standard criteria for sustainable packaging.
  5. What challenges might occur? The challenges in implementing green packaging solutions are high production cost and lack of eco friendly materials
